Web Page Creation and Design Primer

It may be good for the environment that assignments be "handed in" in the form of a webpage, but this also leads to the inevitable question about how one should build a webpage, leading to the creation of this small guide. It is by no means comprehensive, but with a little luck it should be enough to get you started, and provide a general understanding of exactly what a webpage is, and how you might go about building one of your own.
